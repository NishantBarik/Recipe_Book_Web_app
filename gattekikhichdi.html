gatte ki khichdi

Ingredients:

  • Cooked basmati rice 2 1/2 cups
  • Gram flour (besan) 1/2 cup
  • Oil 1/2 tablespoon
  • Red chilli powder 2 teaspoons
  • Salt to taste
  • Carom seeds (ajwain) 1/4 teaspoon
  • Ghee 2 tablespoons
  • Mustard seeds 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin seeds 1 tablespoon
  • Cashewnuts 10-12
  • Bay leaves 2
  • Cloves 3-4
  • Cinnamon 1 inch stick
  • Green cardamoms 2
  • Curry leaves 10-12
  • Green peas boiled 1/4 cup
  • Garam masala powder 1/2 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder 1/2 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder 1/4 teaspoon
  • Salt to taste
  • Lemon juice 1 tablespoon
  • Fresh coriander leaves

Instructions:

  1. To make the gattas mix besan, oil, one teaspoon red chilli powder, salt and ajwain with one tablespoon water to form a hard dough. Divide into equal potions
  2. Roll each portion and shape them into the size of fingers. Heta four cups of water and bring ito boil. Add the rolls and cook them till done.
  3. Remove and cut them into one and a half inch pieces. Keep aside.
  4. Heat ghee in a non-stick pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, cashewnuts, bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon stick, green cardamoms and curry leaves and saute for a minute.
  5. Add the gattas, peas, remaining red chilli powder, garam masala powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, salt and lemon juice and saute for a minute.
  6. Add the rice and mix well. Serve hot garnished with coriander leaves.
