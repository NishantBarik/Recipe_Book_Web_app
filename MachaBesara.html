Macha Besara Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Fish (Desi Rohu) - 500 gm
  • Yogurt ( Dahi) - 4 tbsp
  • Chopped onion - 2 tbsp
  • Garlic clove (Pressed) - 6
  • Yellow mustard ( paste) - 3 to 4 tbsp
  • Green chili (slit) - 2
  • Onion seeds (kala jeera / Kalonji) - ½ tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander stem - 2 tbsp
  • Coriander leaves (chopped) - 1 tbsp
  • Oil - 3 tbsp
  • Salt to taste

    • For Paste : -

  • Yellow mustard seeds - 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder - ¼ tsp
  • Mustard seeds - ½ tsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
  • Garlic clove - 6
  • Red chili - 3
  • Little water

Instructions:

  1. Clean and wash the fishes under running water. Add salt and turmeric powder, mix well and keep it aside.
  2. Take yellow mustard seeds, turmeric powder, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, garlic clove, red chili and add little water and make fine paste with the help of a grinder.
  3. Take out the paste in bowl and add yogurt. Mix it thoroughly and keep it aside.
  4. Heat oil in a kadai / pan. Fry the fishes till the fishes turn golden in colour. Take out the fried fishes and keep it aside.
  5. Heat little oil in another Pan. Add onion seeds (kalajeera / Kalonji). Allow it crackle. Add slit green chili and chopped onion and stir fry for a while.
  6. Then slowly add ¼ cup of water, yellow mustard paste (Besara), coriander stem, pressed garlic and mustard oil (1 tsp). Stir fry it till the water evaporates.
  7. Add (1 cup) boiled water, salt and fried fish pieces and cover it.
  8. Cook the gravy till the desired amount of gravy you need is left.
  9. Open the lid and add chopped coriander leaves.
  10. Serve it with steamed rice or pakhala bhata.
