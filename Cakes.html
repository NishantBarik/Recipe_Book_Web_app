Chocolate Cake

Ingredients required : -

  • Refined flour - 2 cups
  • Butter - 1/2 cup
  • Powdered sugar - 1/2 cup
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Condensed milk - 1/2 cup
  • Baking powder - 1.5 tsp
  • Baking soda - 1/2 tsp

Instructions:-

  1. To prepare cake, take refined flour in a bowl and mix baking powder, baking soda and cocoa powder to it. Mix everything reall well and sieve this mixture twice to mix everything really well.
  2. In another bowl take slightly melted butter and powdered sugar and whisk them thoroughly. Add condensed milk as well and whisk again thoroughly. When the mixture gets puffy add milk to it and mix. Then add refined flour-cocoa powder mixture and milk in small portions and keep mixing well. Batter for cake is ready.bowl, put almonds and pistachios on top and keep it for free for some time
  3. Grease the cake mold container with some butter. Also grease a round shaped butter paper equal to the size of container with butter a s well and place it inside the container. After this, pour cake batter in the mold, tap it over the surface and even it out.
  4. Bake the cake. Preheat oven at 180 degree centigrade and place the container in the middle rack. Now bake the cake for 25 minutes on same temperature.
  5. After 25 minutes, check the cake. If the cake is not baked through then bake again for 10 minutes on 170 degree.
  6. Check the cake again. For this insert a knife inside the cake, it should come out clean Let the cake cool down. Cake has now cooled down, run knife all around the cake to separate i from the container. Place a plate above the container and flip it upside down. Cake will come out on the plate.
  7. Spongy and super yummy eggless spongy cake is ready. Place it chocolate cake in freezer for 10 to 12 days and relish eating.

Vanilla Cheesecake

Ingredients required : -

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • filling:
  • 4 packages (8 ounces each ) cream cheese, softened
  • 1-1/2 cups sugar
  • 3 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature, lightly beaten

Instructions:-

  1. Preheat oven to 325°. Mix cracker crumbs, butter and sugar; press onto bottom and 1 in. up sides of a greased 9-in. springform pan.
  2. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Beat in vanilla and salt. Add eggs; beat on low speed just until blended. Pour into crust. Place on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake until center is almost set, 55-60 minutes. Cool on a wire rack 10 minutes. Loosen sides from pan with a knife. Cool 1 hour longer. Refrigerate overnight, covering when completely cooled.

Strawberry Cake

Ingredients required : -

  • Sugar: This strawberry cake starts with two cups of white sugar.
  • Butter: Cream the sugar with two sticks of softened butter.
  • Jell-O: You'll need a 3-ounce package of strawberry-flavored Jell-O mix.
  • eggs: Four eggs add richness, moisture, and flavor.
  • Flour: This light and fluffy strawberry cake calls for cake flour instead of all-purpose flour.
  • Baking powder: Baking powder acts as a leavener, which means it helps the cake rise.
  • Milk: Whole milk lends moisture to the homemade strawberry cake.
  • Strawberries: Use frozen strawberries to make homemade strawberry purée.
  • Vanilla: Vanilla extract enhances the overall flavor of the cake.

instructions:-

  1. Beat the butter, sugar, and Jell-O together. Add the eggs.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients.
  3. Gradually the dry mixture into the wet mixture, alternating with the milk.
  4. Add the remaining ingredients and transfer the batter to prepared cake pans.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick comes out clean.

Iced Fairy Cake

Ingredients required : -

  • 100g caster sugar
  • 100g very soft butter
  • 100g self-raising flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • For the icing
  • 200g very soft butter
  • 200g icing sugar
  • food colouring, sprinkles, marshmallows etc

Instructions:-

  1. Ask a grown-up helper to turn the oven on to 180C/160C fan/gas 4. Put a paper case in hole of a 12-hole bun tin.
  2. Put the sugar and butter (it must be soft or you won’t be able to mix it properly) in a bowl and mix it together. Sift in the flour.
  3. Break the eggs into a separate bowl (spoon out any bits of shell that fall in) and add them to the bowl with the vanilla. Mix everything together.
  4. Divide between the cases using a spoon, scraping it off with a knife. Ask a grown-up helper to put the tray in the oven for 20 minutes.
  5. Mix the butter and icing sugar to make a creamy icing. Add colouring, if you like. Push an icing nozzle into an icing bag, then scoop in the icing.
  6. Let the cakes cool completely in the tray. Pipe icing onto each cake and decorate with marshmallows or sprinkles, or whatever you like.
