kodi pulao Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 750 gms chicken
  • 2 tbsps oil
  • 2 tbsps ghee
  • 3/4 cup yogut/curd
  • 1 cup sambar onions/shallots
  • 3 bay leaf
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 3 peppers
  • 2 cinnamon stick
  • 3-4 cardamom/ilachi
  • 2 star mace/javitri
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves
  • 1 tbsp chill paste
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2 salt

Instructions:

  1. pre-preparation;
  2. Wash and rinse the basmati rice thoroughly and soak them for 20 mins
  3. Wash and clean the chicken, keep it separately in a bowl.
  4. Heat the oil and ghee a pot in
  5. Fry the cashew nuts in the pot until they turn golden brown. keep them separately in a bowl
  6. Add onions to the pot and sauté until they are golden brown
  7. Add bay leaf, cloves, pepper, cinnamon stick, cardamom, star anise/Chakri fool, javitri/stand mace and sauté them for two minutes.
  8. Then add green chili paste and ginger garlic paste. Saute until the raw flavor is gone.
  9. Then add chicken and mix well
  10. Add ¾ cup of yogurt and stir well.
  11. Add salt, chili powder, and garam masala and mix well
  12. Add coriander leaves and mint leaves, stir well and cover the lid
  13. Cook on a medium flame for 20 mins or else until the oil separates from the chicken.
  14. Then add 3 cups of water and boil the water
  15. Then finally add soaked basmati rice and stir well and cover the lid and cook for 15 mins.
  16. Open the lid and add cashew nuts to the rice and mix carefully. Then cover the lid and cook for 10 mins.
  17. Serve the hot raju gari Kodi pulao with raita.
