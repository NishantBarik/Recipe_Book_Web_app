Palak Paneer Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach (palak)
  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions, green chilies, ginger, and garlic. Saut√© until onions are golden brown.
  3. Stir in tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  4. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add fresh spinach and cook until it wilts.
  6. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5-7 minutes until the flavors blend.
  7. Palak Paneer is ready to be served. Garnish with cream if desired.
