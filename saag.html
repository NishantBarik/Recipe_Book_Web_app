Sarson Da Saag Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch mustard leaves / sarson ke patte
  • ½ bunch spinach / palak
  • 2 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 inch ginger, chopped
  • ¼ onion, chopped
  • 2 chilli
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cornmeal / makki ka atta
  • 2 tbsp ghee / clarified butter
  • pinch hing / asafoetida
  • 2 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 inch ginger, finely chopped
  • 1 chilli, slit
  • 1 onion, finely chopped

Instructions:

  1. firstly, wash and finely chop 1 bunch mustard leaves (sarson ke patte) and ½ bunch spinach (palak). also add bathua leaves if available.
  2. take the chopped leaves in pressure cooker along with 2 clove garlic, 1 inch ginger, ¼ onion, 2 chilli and ½ tsp salt.
  3. add 1 cup water and pressure cook for 4 whistles.
  4. mash until coarsely ground.
  5. further add 2 tbsp of makki ka atta and mix well.
  6. cook for 5 minutes or until the mixture thickens. keep aside.
  7. now in a large kadai, heat 2 tbsp ghee, and saute 2 clove garlic, 1 inch ginger and 1 green chilli.
  8. further saute 1 onion until it turns golden brown.
  9. add in cooked and mashed leaves and mix well.
  10. continue to cook for 4-5 minutes, or until the saag is cooked completely.
