Gulab Jamun

Ingredients required : -

  1. Mawa (khoya) - 250 gm
  2. Paneer (cottage cheese) - 100 gm
  3. Refined flour - 100 gm
  4. Cashew Nuts - 15 to 16 (cut each cashew in 8 small pieces)
  5. Dried Raisins - 25 to 30
  6. Sugar - 600 gm
  7. Ghee - 250 gm

instructions:-

  • Put the mawa (khoya), paneer and refined flour in a flatted big utensil and knead them till they look like.
  • Boil the oil in a frying pan.
  • Take out a small lump (about one tea spoon) of the mawa (khoya) dough and flatten it using your palms. Put 3 to 4 small pieces of cashews along with a dry raisin, on it and enclose it from all the four corners. Now keep it between the palms and turn it into a round ball like shape.
  • Put 3 to 4 of these ball shaped dough in the frying pan (fry them on medium flame and ensure that frying spoon doesn't touch them.
  • Put hot ghee on them with the frying spoon. Overturn them when they turn light brown. Like this fry them to light brown color from all sides).
  • Now take out the gulab jamuns in a plate. After 2 minutes submerge them in the sweet syrup. Like this make all the ball shaped gulab jamuns and after frying submerge them in the sweet syrup.

Gajar Ka Halwa

Ingredients required : -

  1. Orange carrot- 4 (250 gms)
  2. Almonds- 7-8 (chopped)
  3. Cashew- 7-8 (chopped)
  4. Sugar- ⅓ cup (80 gms)
  5. Ghee- 2 tbsp
  6. Full cream milk- ½ litre
  7. Raisins- 1 tbsp
  8. Green cardamom- 4

instructions:-

  • Peel the cardamoms and make the powder of its seeds.
  • Preheat the pan and add 2 tbsp of ghee in it to melt. Add the grated carrot in ghee and roast it on the low flame.Roast it for the 2-3 minutes. Add the ½ cup of warm milk in the carrot after the 3 minutes. Cover the carrot and cook it on a medium flame for the 5 minutes. Cook the carrot until it is soft and stir it in once in between of the cooking process.
  • After the 5 minutes, add the whole milk in carrot and mix them. Cook the carrot halwa on high flame until it boils and stirs it continuously. Stop the continuous stirring once the halwa boils. Check and cook the halwa for 2-3 minutes. Cook the halwa until it is thick.
  • After cooking the halwa for 10 minutes on high flame add raisins in it and mix them. Cook it more on high flame until it is thick. Once, the halwa is thick, add the chopped almonds, chopped cashew and sugar in it. Stir the halwa for a while and then halwa is ready. Cool down the halwa for a while and then take it out on a plate. Garnish it with the chopped dry fruits.

Falooda

Ingredients required : -

  1. 1 tin Nestlé MILKMAID
  2. 2 tbsp Sabja Seeds (Basil Seeds / Tukmalanga)
  3. 6 tbsp Vermicelli
  4. 12 tsp Rose Syrup
  5. 6 cups Crushed Ice
  6. For Garnish 1 tbsp Dry Fruits

instructions:-

  • Soak sabja seeds in excess water for 1 hr. Cook vermicelli in boiling water for 3-4 min. Strain and keep aside.
  • Take a 220 ml tall glass and add 1 tsp rose syrup at the bottom. Top it with 1 tbsp cooked vermicelli followed by 1 tbsp sabja seeds, 1 tbsp Nestlé MILKMAID, and 5 tbsp crushed ice.
  • Repeat the above step and garnish with dry fruits.
  • Mix well and serve.

Shrikhand

Ingredients required : -

  1. 2 cups yogurt
  2. 1/4 cup sugar
  3. 1/4 cup milk
  4. 10 saffron
  5. 4 small cardamom
  6. 3-4 pistachios
  7. 4 almonds

instructions:-

  • To make Shrikhand, first, take curd and tie it in a clean cloth and keep it in a place for some time.
  • Now take a cloth and press it by hand and set aside all the curd water. Take milk in a vessel and mix saffron in it.
  • After doing this, take out the curd in a bowl and add cardamom and sugar to it and mix well. Add saffron milk to this mixture and mix well and beat it.
  • Now add chopped pistachios and almond pieces and mix all the mixture. Your Shrikhand is ready.
  • Take it out in a bowl, put almonds and pistachios on top and keep it for free for some time

Indian Rice Pudding(Kheer)

Ingredients required : -

  1. Full-cream Milk - 1 liter
  2. Rice - ¼ cup (50 grams)
  3. Dry coconut - 2 tsp (grated)
  4. Almond - 2 tsp
  5. Raisin - 2 tsp
  6. Cardamom powder -½ tsp
  7. Sugar - ¼ cup (50 grams)

instructions:-

  • To prepare rice kheer, take a pot with a thick bottom. I If you are making kheer with 1-liter milk then, use a bigger pot that can hold 2 liters of milk. Wash the pot thoroughly with water.
  • To prepare rice kheer, take a pot with a thick bottom. I Stir the milk at regular intervals, until the milk thickens, and rice mixes up with milk. Stir the kheer every 1-2 minutes.
  • To prepare rice kheer, take a pot with a thick bottom. I While cooking, you will notice cream floating above the milk. You have to dissolve the cream in the milk every time it forms To make the milk thicker.
  • To prepare rice kheer, take a pot with a thick bottom. I Rice is cooked but still needs to be mixed up with milk. So, we can add dried fruits now. Add 2 tsp of finely chopped coconut, 2 tsp of almonds, and 2 tsp of raisins. Mix and cook everything while stirring it at intervals.
