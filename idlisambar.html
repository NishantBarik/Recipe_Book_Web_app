Idli Sambar

Ingredients:

  • For Idli:
  • Idli rice 2 cups
  • Urd dal 1/2 cup
  • Poha 1/4 cup
  • Salt to taste
  • For Sambhar:
  • Arhar ki dal 1/2 cup
  • Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil 2 tsp
  • Mustard seeds 1/2 tsp
  • Curry leaves 10-12
  • Onion 1 cup (chopped)
  • Tomato 1 cup (chopped)
  • Sambhar masala 2
  • Brinjal 1 (chopped)
  • Tamarind pulp 2 tbsp

Instructions:

  1. For Idli:
  2. Wash and soak rice poha and urd dal separately for 4-5 hours.
  3. Drain and blend in a blender together with salt and poha till smooth.
  4. Transfer the batter in a big vessel and cover and keep to ferment for 6-8 hours.
  5. Grease the idli molds and pour the batter in each mold.
  6. Steam the idlies for 15-20 minutes, till done.
  7. For Sambhar:
  8. Wash the dal.
  9. Add it in a pressure cooker with salt and turmeric powder.
  10. Cook till done.
  11. Add oil in a pan.
  12. When the oil is hot, add mustard seeds and curry leaves.
  13. Add onion and fry for 2 minutes.
  14. Add tomatoes, brinjal, and sambhar masala.
  15. Add half a cup of water and cook till brinjal is soft.
  16. Add the cooked dal and tamarind pulp.
  17. Cook for another 4-5 minutes.
  18. Serve hot with idlies.
